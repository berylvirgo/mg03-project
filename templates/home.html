About

There's is a beginning to every story. This was where ours began.

  • July 2021

    Application and promotion phase

    Google and Andela took 65,000 people in for this amazing program. The program consists of 3 tracks (Android Developer, Cloud Engineer, Mobile Web Specialist). Our program materials where provided on Pluralsight.

  • September 2021

    Learning Phase 1

    For all tracks, we had to receive 100+ for the track-specific Skill IQ OR complete 10+ hours of track-specific content.

  • November 2021

    Learning Phase 2

    For all tracks, we had to receive 200+ for the track-specific Skill IQ OR complete 20+ hours of track-specific content.

  • December 2021

    Project Phase

    We were required to come up with a solution that will resolve the disruption caused by the pandemic in these different industries (Education, Health Sector, Fintech Space) using these specific APIs (Strava, Spotify, Paypal, Udemy, Flutterwave).
